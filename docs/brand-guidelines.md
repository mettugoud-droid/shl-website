- Usage: Headers, primary text, backgrounds, brand elements

## Logo Usage

### Placement Rules
- Minimum clear space: 1x logo height on all sides
- Minimum size: 120px width
- Do not stretch, distort, or recolor
- Use on white or dark navy backgrounds only

### Logo Variations
1. Full logo (icon + wordmark) – Primary use
2. Icon only – Favicon, small spaces
3. Wordmark only – Where icon is not needed

---

## Photography Style
- Real trucks and warehouses (avoid stock feel)
- Blue-toned color grading
- Professional, clean composition
- Show real operations and team (when available)

---

## Iconography
- Style: Lucide Icons (line style)
- Weight: 1.5px stroke
- Size: 24px default, 20px small, 32px large
- Color: Match surrounding text or use secondary orange for highlights

---

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional** but not stiff
- **Confident** without being arrogant
- **Clear** and jargon-free
- **Action-oriented** language
- **Customer-focused** messaging

### Do Say:
- "We deliver on time, every time"
- "Your trusted logistics partner"
- "Efficient. Reliable. Professional."

### Don't Say:
- "Cheap transport services"
- "We try our best"
- Generic corporate speak
